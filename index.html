Career roadmap

What should I do each year?

A simple roadmap for students who want to prepare early and avoid feeling lost.

Year 1

Freshman Year

Explore majors, join student clubs, build LinkedIn, attend events, and learn Excel or coding basics.

Year 2

Sophomore Year

Start networking, apply to early programs, build technical skills, and talk to upperclassmen.

Year 3

Junior Year

Apply for internships, prepare for interviews, improve your resume, and do targeted coffee chats.

Year 4

Senior Year

Recruit for full-time roles, compare offers, stay connected with alumni, and mentor younger students.
